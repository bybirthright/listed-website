FAQ

How do I add Listed to my server?
Adding Listed to your server is easy! Simply click on the "Get Started" button at the top of this page, and follow the prompts to authorize the bot for your server. You'll need to have the "Manage Server" permission to add bots to your Discord server.
Is Listed free to use?
Listed will always be free to use. However, we do offer premium features such as customization, auction and more. Check out the premium page for more details.
How does creating listings work?
Creating listings for your products or services is as simple as running one command and following the easy-to-use prompts that the bot will generate for you. You can also update listings or delete them as needed. All permissions for channels will be handled by the bot to keep your server safe.
Can Listed help prevent scams?
Listed has a reputation system for all users. Trusted sellers can build a reputation that can be easily checked by any user and will help users in preventing scams. Listed bot is not liable for any scams that may occur through sales advertised by Listed. Please always use your initiative and stay safe.
